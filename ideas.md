## Idea 1: "Cyber-Zenith" - The Futuristic Terminal

**Design Movement**: Cyberpunk / High-Tech Minimalist
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Data-First Immersion**: The interface feels like a heads-up display (HUD) where data is beautiful and primary.
2.  **Neon & Void**: Deep, infinite backgrounds contrasted with sharp, glowing accents.
3.  **Precision Engineering**: Every interaction feels mechanical, precise, and instant.
4.  **Focus Tunnel**: The layout drives all attention to the center, simulating a "flow state" tunnel.

**Color Philosophy**:
*   **Background**: Deepest Navy/Black (`#050A14`) - Represents the void of space or a powered-down screen, minimizing eye strain.
*   **Primary Text**: Cool Silver/Blue (`#A0B0C0`) - Readable, clinical, and calm.
*   **Accents**: Electric Cyan (`#00F0FF`) and Laser Gold (`#FFD700`) - Used sparingly for active states and high scores, creating a "power-up" feeling.
*   **Error**: Glitch Red (`#FF003C`) - Sharp and immediate feedback.

**Layout Paradigm**:
*   **HUD-Style**: Stats are floating elements, not boxed. Navigation is minimal and tucked away.
*   **Central Stage**: The typing area is the absolute center, with other elements fading out when typing starts.

**Signature Elements**:
1.  **Scanlines & Glow**: Subtle CRT scanline overlay on the background and glow effects on active keys.
2.  **Monospaced Mastery**: Heavy use of monospaced fonts for everything, not just the typing test.
3.  **Data Visualizers**: Real-time graphs that look like system diagnostics.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
*   **Instant Feedback**: Keys light up immediately. Transitions are sharp cuts or very fast slides (100ms).
*   **Sound**: Mechanical keyboard clicks (optional) and subtle hums.

**Animation**:
*   **Glitch Effects**: On errors or mode switches.
*   **Cursor**: A solid block cursor that blinks or slides smoothly.
*   **Entrance**: Elements "boot up" or slide in from the edges like a system initialization.

**Typography System**:
*   **Primary**: `JetBrains Mono` or `Roboto Mono` - Technical, legible, code-like.
*   **Headings**: `Orbitron` or `Rajdhani` - Futuristic, angular.
</text>

## Idea 2: "Deep Focus" - The Ethereal Flow

**Design Movement**: Glassmorphism / Soft UI
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Cognitive Ease**: Remove all visual friction. Soft edges, blurred backgrounds, and gentle transitions.
2.  **Fluidity**: The interface feels like liquid; typing creates ripples of activity.
3.  **Atmospheric Depth**: Layers of transparency create a sense of floating in a calm space.
4.  **Organic Rhythm**: The pacing of animations matches a resting heartbeat.

**Color Philosophy**:
*   **Background**: Dark Slate/Charcoal (`#1E293B`) with subtle, moving gradients of deep indigo and violet.
*   **Primary Text**: Soft White/Mist (`#E2E8F0`) - High contrast but not harsh.
*   **Accents**: Soft Coral (`#F472B6`) and Mint (`#34D399`) - Friendly, encouraging colors for progress.
*   **Glass**: Translucent panels with background blur (`backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`).

**Layout Paradigm**:
*   **Floating Cards**: Content lives on semi-transparent layers floating above the background.
*   **Asymmetric Balance**: Menu on the left, stats on the right, typing in the center but breathing room everywhere.

**Signature Elements**:
1.  **Frosted Glass**: Used for the keyboard overlay and modals.
2.  **Ambient Glow**: A soft, moving light source behind the active word.
3.  **Rounded Everything**: Super-rounded corners (`rounded-3xl`) to soften the tech feel.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
*   **Elasticity**: Buttons and keys have a slight "squish" or bounce when pressed.
*   **Flow**: Transitions are slow and smooth (300-500ms), emphasizing continuity.

**Animation**:
*   **Ripple Effects**: Typing correct words sends a subtle ripple through the background or progress bar.
*   **Fade & Float**: Elements drift into place rather than snapping.
*   **Cursor**: A smooth, trailing caret (like a comet tail).

**Typography System**:
*   **Primary**: `Inter` or `Nunito` - Rounded, friendly, highly readable.
*   **Headings**: `Quicksand` - Approachable and soft.

## Idea 3: "Typewriter Noir" - The Modern Classic

**Design Movement**: Neo-Brutalism / Retro-Modern
**Core Principles**:
1.  **Tactile Nostalgia**: Evokes the feeling of a physical typewriter but with modern speed.
2.  **High Contrast & Boldness**: Stark differences between background and text. No gradients, just solid colors.
3.  **Raw Functionality**: Exposed structural elements (borders, grids) used as design features.
4.  **Rhythm & Percussion**: Visuals emphasize the rhythmic nature of typing.

**Color Philosophy**:
*   **Background**: Very Dark Grey/Ink (`#121212`) - Like the ribbon of a typewriter.
*   **Primary Text**: Off-White/Paper (`#F5F5F5`) - Crisp and clear.
*   **Accents**: International Orange (`#FF4500`) and Signal Yellow (`#FFD700`) - Industrial, warning-label aesthetics.
*   **Borders**: Thin, sharp grey lines defining every section.

**Layout Paradigm**:
*   **Grid-Based**: Visible grid lines separating the header, typing area, and footer.
*   **Modular**: Each component (timer, wpm, settings) lives in its own clearly defined box.

**Signature Elements**:
1.  **Hard Shadows**: Buttons and cards have solid, non-blurred drop shadows (offset by 4px).
2.  **Monochrome Icons**: Simple, thick-lined icons.
3.  **Raw Borders**: 1px or 2px solid borders on everything.

**Interaction Philosophy**:
*   **Snap & Click**: Interactions are instant and rigid. No bounce, just state changes.
*   **Mechanical**: Hover effects invert colors or shift the element position sharply.

**Animation**:
*   **Typewriter Scroll**: The active line stays fixed while previous lines scroll up sharply.
*   **Cursor**: A solid block that doesn't blink, just moves.
*   **Step Animation**: Progress bars fill in discrete steps (per word) rather than smoothly.

**Typography System**:
*   **Primary**: `Courier Prime` or `Space Mono` - The quintessential typewriter look.
*   **Headings**: `Chivo Mono` or `Archivo Black` - Heavy, industrial, impactful.
</text>
